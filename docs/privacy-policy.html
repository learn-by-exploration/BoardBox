Privacy Policy

Board Box  ·  Effective date: June 2025  ·  Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Board Box ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. Board Box is a collection of classic local multiplayer board games (Gomoku, Othello, Checkers, Dots & Boxes, and Tic Tac Toe) designed for all ages, including children.

✓ We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information.

Board Box operates entirely on your device. The only data the app stores are:

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, draws) saved locally on your device via Android's SharedPreferences storage.
  • App settings (theme, hints, haptics) saved locally on your device.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. Internet & Network Access

Board Box does not make any network requests. The INTERNET permission listed in the Android manifest is a standard declaration retained for potential future features, but is currently unused. No data is sent over the network.

3. Advertising

No ads

Board Box contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not integrate any advertising SDK, ad network, or monetisation framework.

4. Third-Party SDKs & Services

Board Box integrates only the Flutter framework and the shared_preferences plugin (local device storage only). No analytics, crash-reporting, social-login, advertising, or any other third-party SDK is included in the app.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Board Box is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13.
  • We do not require account registration or login.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not allow in-app purchases.
  • We do not include social features, chat, or user-generated content.
  • All content in Board Box is appropriate for all ages.

✓ COPPA Compliant — no data collected from any user

6. EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Because we collect no personal data, Board Box has no processing activities that require a legal basis under the GDPR. There is no personal data to access, rectify, erase, or port. Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have no data stored by us to exercise rights over.

✓ GDPR Compliant — no personal data processed

7. Google Play Families Policy

Board Box is designed to comply with Google Play's Families Policy:

  • All content is appropriate for children of all ages.
  • The app contains no violence, sexual content, horror, or mature themes.
  • No ads are shown to any user.
  • No data is collected from any user, including children.
  • No third-party SDKs that could compromise children's privacy are integrated.
  • No in-app purchases are present.

8. Data Security

Because Board Box stores only local game statistics and settings on your own device, your data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (encryption, screen lock, etc.). We have no servers and therefore no server-side data that could be breached.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Local data (game stats and settings) is retained on your device until you uninstall the app. Uninstalling Board Box permanently removes all locally stored app data from your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. Significant changes that affect children's data (if we ever begin collecting any) will be communicated via an in-app notice.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Google Play Store listing or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
